Memphis, Saqqara & NMEC Private Day Tour

Step back 5,000 years to the foundations of Ancient Egypt with a licensed archaeology expert.

Journey to the Old Kingdom

Go beyond the surface of history. Our private odyssey begins in **Memphis**, the legendary first capital of unified Egypt. This chapter is essential for those who have already completed the Giza Pyramids tour, as it reveals where the Pharaohs actually lived and designed their eternal legacies.

We then explore **Saqqara**, a vast ancient necropolis home to the Step Pyramid of Djoser—the world's first besar-scale stone architecture. To conclude this profound day, we visit the **NMEC**, where you will enter the Royal Mummies Hall, standing face-to-face with the greatest Kings and Queens of Egypt. This experience elevates your trip far beyond standard Cairo cultural activities.

Tour Intelligence (FAQ)

Is the NMEC museum better than the Giza museum?

Both are majestic. While Giza offers the Pyramids and GEM, NMEC is the only place in the world where you can stand face-to-face with the Royal Mummies in a specialized, modern exhibit.

Are entrance tickets included in the price?

Yes. To ensure a hassle-free VIP experience, all our private tours include pre-paid entrance tickets to all mentioned archaeological sites.
