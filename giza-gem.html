Giza Pyramids & Grand Egyptian Museum Private Tour

An exclusive journey through the last surviving Wonder of the Ancient World and the masterpiece of modern Egypt.

Experience Details

Our signature private tour begins at the Giza Plateau, where your licensed archaeologist guide will unveil the architectural secrets of Khufu, Khafre, and Menkaure. Witness the Sphinx's eternal gaze before transitioning to the world's most anticipated cultural project: The Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM).

Inside the GEM, experience the grandeur of the Ramses II colossus and explore the majestic Grand Staircase, offering a preview of Egypt's unparalleled heritage in a futuristic setting.
