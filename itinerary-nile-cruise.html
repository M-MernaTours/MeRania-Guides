Luxurious 4-Day Private Nile Cruise

Journey into the heart of Upper Egypt. Experience the eternal Nile from Luxor to Aswan with private archaeology experts and five-star full-board comfort.

The Royal Itinerary

Day 1: The Monumental East Bank

Arrival in Luxor and check-in. Explore **Karnak Temple**, the largest religious complex ever built, followed by the majestic **Luxor Temple** at sunset with your private guide. Overnight on the Nile.

Day 2: The Gateway to Afterlife

Cross to the West Bank to discover the **Valley of the Kings**, the **Temple of Hatshepsut**, and the Colossi of Memnon. Set sail towards Edfu, passing through the Esna Lock.

Day 3: Temples of the Gods

Visit the incredibly preserved **Edfu Temple**. Continue sailing to **Kom Ombo** to explore the unique double temple of Sobek and Horus. Sail towards Aswan.

Day 4: Romantic Aswan & Philae

Experience the island beauty of **Philae Temple** and the engineering marvel of the High Dam. Optional early morning visit to **Abu Simbel**. Check-out and private transfer.
