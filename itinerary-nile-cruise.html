Nile Cruise 4 Days / 3 Nights

Experience the magic of the Nile on a luxury cruise sailing from Luxor to Aswan with guided sightseeing.

Tour Overview

This 4-day / 3-night Nile Cruise is one of the most popular ways to explore Upper Egypt. Enjoy a relaxing cruise experience combined with guided tours to Egypt’s most famous temples.

Detailed Itinerary

Day 1 – Luxor

Check-in before lunch. Visit Karnak Temple and Luxor Temple. Dinner and overnight on board.

Day 2 – Luxor to Edfu

Visit the Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple and Colossi of Memnon. Sail to Edfu via Esna Lock.

Day 3 – Edfu to Aswan

Visit Edfu Temple, sail to Kom Ombo and explore the twin temple. Continue sailing to Aswan.

Day 4 – Aswan

Visit Philae Temple and the High Dam. Optional trip to Abu Simbel. Check-out after breakfast.
