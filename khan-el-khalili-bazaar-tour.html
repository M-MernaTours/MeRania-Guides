Frequently Asked Questions

What is Khan El Khalili?

Khan El Khalili is a historic marketplace in Cairo, known for its traditional crafts, spices, and souvenirs.

Is Khan El Khalili worth visiting?

Yes, Khan El Khalili is a must-visit destination in Cairo. It offers a unique glimpse into the city's rich cultural heritage and provides an authentic shopping experience.

How long does the Khan El Khalili tour take?

The tour typically lasts between 2-3 hours, allowing you to explore the various sections of the bazaar and interact with local vendors.

Can I buy souvenirs in Khan El Khalili?

Yes, Khan El Khalili is famous for its traditional crafts, spices, and souvenirs. You can find a wide variety of unique items to take home as mementos.
