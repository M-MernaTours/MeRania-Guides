Alexandria Day Tour from Cairo

Private 12-hour archaeological journey to the Pearl of the Mediterranean.

The Mediterranean Experience

Step away from the desert and breathe the Mediterranean air. Founded by Alexander the Great, Alexandria is a city where history meets the sea. On this private journey, we explore the majestic 15th-century Citadel of Qaitbay, built upon the exact site of the ancient Lighthouse of Alexandria.

You will marvel at the architectural genius of the modern Library of Alexandria and descend into the fascinating Kom El Shoqafa Catacombs. Every step is curated and guided by a licensed Egyptologist for a deep, insightful experience.

Tour Intelligence (FAQ)

How long is the drive from Cairo to Alexandria?

The journey takes approximately 3 hours each way via the desert road, conducted in a modern, private air-conditioned vehicle for your comfort.

Is the tour itinerary flexible?

Yes. As this is a private tour, we can adjust the time spent at each site or add a stop at a seaside cafe based on your preference.

What should I wear for the Alexandria tour?

Comfortable walking shoes are recommended for the Catacombs and Citadel. Modest clothing is best for the library and city walking.
