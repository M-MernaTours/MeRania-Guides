Alexandria Day Tour from Cairo

Discover Egypt’s Mediterranean city on a private full-day tour with a licensed Egyptian tour guide.

Tour Overview

Alexandria is Egypt’s second-largest city and its window to the Mediterranean. This private tour takes you from Cairo to Alexandria to explore its most famous landmarks in one unforgettable day.

What’s Included

  • Private licensed English-speaking tour guide
  • Private air-conditioned vehicle
  • Hotel pickup & drop-off from Cairo or Giza
  • Bottled water during the tour
  • All entrance tickets to mentioned sites

What’s Excluded

• Lunch and personal expenses
• Drinks during lunch
• Tips (optional)

Detailed Itinerary

• Early pickup from your hotel in Cairo or Giza
• Drive to Alexandria (approx. 3 hours)
• Visit the Citadel of Qaitbay
• Visit the Library of Alexandria
• Explore the Corniche & Roman sites
• Return to Cairo in the evening

Price & Notes

✔ Entrance tickets included
✔ Private tour only (not shared)
✔ Comfortable long-distance transportation
✔ Customizable itinerary
