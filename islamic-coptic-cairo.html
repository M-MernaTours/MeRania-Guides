Islamic & Coptic Cairo Day Tour

Explore the spiritual heart of Cairo on a private cultural tour visiting Islamic Cairo and Coptic Cairo with a licensed guide.

Tour Overview

This private day tour takes you through Cairo’s rich religious history. Discover Islamic Cairo’s historic mosques and streets, then explore Coptic Cairo, home to some of the oldest churches in Egypt.

What’s Included

  • Private licensed speaking tour guide
  • Private air-conditioned vehicle
  • Hotel pickup & drop-off
  • Bottled water during the tour
  • All entrance tickets to mentioned sites

What’s Excluded

• Lunch and personal expenses
• Drinks during lunch
• Tips (optional)

Detailed Itinerary

• Pickup from your hotel in Cairo or Giza
• Visit Salah El-Din Citadel (optional)
• Explore historic mosques of Islamic Cairo
• Walk through ancient streets and markets
• Visit Coptic Cairo churches (Hanging Church, Abu Serga)
• Return to hotel
