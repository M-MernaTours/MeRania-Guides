Islamic & Coptic Cairo Day Tour

Explore the religious and cultural heart of Cairo in one private day tour.

Tour Overview

This private day tour takes you through the spiritual heart of Cairo. Discover Islamic Cairo’s historic mosques and Coptic Cairo’s ancient churches, all guided by a licensed Egyptian tour guide.

What’s Included

  • Private licensed speaking tour guide
  • Private air-conditioned vehicle
  • Hotel pickup & drop-off
  • Bottled water
  • All entrance tickets included

What’s Excluded

• Lunch & personal expenses
• Drinks
• Tips (optional)

Detailed Itinerary

• Pickup from hotel
• Visit Islamic Cairo (Citadel / Sultan Hassan / Al-Rifa’i)
• Walk through historic Islamic streets
• Visit Coptic Cairo (Hanging Church, St. Sergius, Coptic Museum)
• Return to hotel
