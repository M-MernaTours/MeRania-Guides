Islamic & Coptic Cairo Private Day Tour

A spiritual journey through the minarets and ancient spires of Old Cairo.

The Soul of Egypt

Step into the living history of Cairo. Our private tour takes you through the multi-layered heritage of the city. We begin in **Islamic Cairo**, exploring the Saladin Citadel and the architectural majesty of the Sultan Hassan Mosque. This is the perfect companion to our Memphis & Saqqara tour for those wanting to see how Cairo evolved through the ages.

We then transition to **Coptic Cairo**, where we visit the famous Hanging Church and the cavern church of St. Sergius, which sheltered the Holy Family. To truly experience the city's pulse, we walk through the vibrant alleys of the old city, a highlight of our Cairo cultural experiences.
