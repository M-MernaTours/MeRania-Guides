8 Days / 7 Nights – Cairo + Luxor & Aswan

A complete private Egypt itinerary combining Cairo’s top highlights with Luxor & Aswan. Choose the classic hotel-based plan or upgrade to a Nile Cruise option (dates dependent).

Private Tour Licensed Guides Flexible Planning Optional Nile Cruise

Best For

First-time visitors who want a complete Egypt experience without rushing.

Trip Style

Private guide + private car. Add flights/train and Nile Cruise as an option.

Tickets

Entrance tickets can be included upon request. You’ll get a clear final quote on WhatsApp.

Detailed Itinerary (8 Days)

1

Arrival in Cairo

  • Meet & assist (optional) + private transfer to hotel
  • Rest day or evening walk (optional)
2

Cairo: Giza Pyramids & GEM

  • Giza Pyramids, Sphinx & panoramic viewpoint
  • Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM) guided visit
  • Back to hotel
3

Cairo: Memphis – Saqqara – NMEC

  • Memphis open-air museum
  • Saqqara Step Pyramid complex
  • NMEC (Royal Mummies Hall optional)
4

Fly/Train to Luxor + East Bank

  • Transfer to airport/train station (depending on your choice)
  • Karnak Temple + Luxor Temple
  • Overnight in Luxor (or board Nile Cruise if chosen)
5

Luxor: West Bank

  • Valley of the Kings
  • Hatshepsut Temple + Colossi of Memnon
  • Optional: tomb upgrades based on availability
6

Aswan + Optional Nile Cruise Day

  • Transfer to Aswan (or cruise sailing schedule)
  • Philae Temple
  • Optional: Felucca ride at sunset
7

Abu Simbel (Optional) / Free Day

  • Optional early trip to Abu Simbel
  • Or free time for relaxation, shopping, or Nubian experience
8

Return / Departure

  • Private transfer to airport/train station
  • End of services

What’s Included

  • Licensed private tour guide
  • Private air-conditioned transportation
  • Hotel pickup & drop-off (within itinerary cities)
  • Bottled water during tours
  • Support for planning, timing & customization

What’s Excluded

  • Hotel accommodation (can be arranged on request)
  • Domestic flights / sleeper train (Cairo–Luxor/Aswan)
  • Meals & drinks (unless included in your chosen cruise)
  • Tips (optional)
