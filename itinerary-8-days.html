8-Day Private Grand Egypt Odyssey

A masterfully designed private journey spanning Cairo, Luxor, and Aswan. Experience the absolute soul of Ancient Egypt with bespoke archaeology guides and seamless luxury coordination.

Days 1–3: The Old Kingdom & Culture (Cairo)

  • Private expedition to Giza Pyramids, the Sphinx, and the Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM).
  • Discovery of Saqqara Step Pyramid and the Royal Mummies Hall at the NMEC.
  • Cultural journey through Saladin's Citadel and the ancient streets of Old Cairo.

Days 4–6: The Golden Age of Pharaohs (Luxor)

  • The spiritual majesty of Karnak Temple and Luxor Temple on the East Bank.
  • The mysteries of the Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple, and Colossi of Memnon.
  • Optional Sunrise Hot Air Balloon flight for a panoramic Nile experience.

Days 7–8: The Sun & The River (Aswan)

  • The romantic island pilgrimage to Philae Temple and the High Dam.
  • Iconic early morning expedition to the monumental Great Temples of Abu Simbel.
  • Private Nile Felucca sailing and VIP coordination for your final departure.

Itinerary Intelligence (FAQ)

Can we include a Nile Cruise in this 8-day trip?

Absolutely. Most of our guests choose to spend 3 or 4 nights on a luxury Nile Cruise between Luxor and Aswan during Days 4-7. We can seamlessly coordinate this with your private guides.

Do you help with domestic flights?

Yes. For this grand tour, we recommend flying between Cairo and Luxor/Aswan to maximize your time. We can assist in coordinating these flights as part of your package.
