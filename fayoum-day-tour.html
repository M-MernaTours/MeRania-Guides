Fayoum Safari & Wadi El Hitan Private Tour

Escape the city and discover the wild, ancient secrets of the Sahara.

The Sahara Odyssey

Ready for a different kind of magic? Our private Fayoum expedition is an immersive journey into Egypt's natural soul. We begin at **Wadi El Rayan**, exploring the majestic waterfalls in the middle of the desert. For those who enjoyed the Giza Pyramids tour, this safari provides a breathtaking contrast to the stone monuments.

We then head deep into the dunes in a professional 4x4 vehicle to reach **Wadi El Hitan** (Valley of the Whales), a UNESCO World Heritage site where prehistoric fossils tell the story of the earth's evolution. This adventure is one of our top-rated Cairo cultural experiences for nature lovers.

Absolutely. As with all our private packages, all site entrance fees are pre-paid and included in the price.
