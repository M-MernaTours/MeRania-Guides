Why Alexandria is Different

Alexandria is not just a city; it's an experience. From the moment you step into the bustling streets, you're immersed in a unique blend of Mediterranean charm and Egyptian culture. The city's rich history, from the legendary Lighthouse to the modern Bibliotheca Alexandrina, offers a diverse range of sights that cater to all interests.

With our Alexandria Private Day Tour, you don't just see the city; you feel it. We navigate the streets in professional vehicles, ensuring a safe yet thrilling ride for families and solo adventurers alike.

What to Expect on Your Day Trip

Breathtaking Mediterranean Views: Walking along the Alexandria Corniche offers a refreshing escape from the bustle of Cairo, with the sea breeze and stunning views of the Eastern Harbor.

A Journey Through Time: You will explore the legendary Catacombs of Kom El Shoqafa, an architectural wonder blending Egyptian and Roman styles, and stand where the Great Lighthouse once stood at the Qaitbay Citadel.

Culinary Delights: No Alexandria tour is complete without a gourmet seafood lunch. We hand-pick restaurants that offer the freshest "catch of the day" with a direct view of the sea.

Alexandria Travel Checklist:

  • Comfortable Footwear: There is a fair amount of walking involved, especially inside the Citadel and the Library.
  • Layered Clothing: Alexandria is cooler than Cairo; even in summer, the sea breeze can be chilly in the evening.
  • Digital/Credit Cards: Remember that major sites like the Library and Catacombs are now cashless for entrance fees.
