Policies & Processes

Our Processes & Policies

  • Free cancellation is allowed up to 48 hours before the tour.
  • Refunds will be processed for valid cancellations according to our policy.
  • Guests must follow our safety guidelines during tours.
  • Last-minute cancellations may incur partial charges.
  • We respect privacy and data protection of all customers.
  • Special requests should be communicated at least 72 hours in advance.
  • We reserve the right to modify tour itineraries for safety or operational reasons.
  • Feedback is welcomed to improve our services.
  • Misbehavior or disregard for instructions may result in removal from the tour without refund.
  • Tours accommodate most mobility requirements, but please inform us of any special needs in advance.
  • We may take photos during tours for promotional purposes.
  • Guests who do not wish to be photographed should inform the guide.
