Luxor East Bank Private Tour

An exclusive journey through the temples and tombs on the east bank of the Nile in Luxor.

Discover the Grandeur of Luxor’s East Bank

Embark on a journey through the "City of a Hundred Gates" and witness the monumental legacy of the Ancient Egyptian Empire. The East Bank of Luxor is where history breathes, home to the most massive temple complexes ever built by human hands.

and the final resting places of pharaohs and queens. With Merna & Rania, you will experience this archaeological wonderland with the insight and expertise of licensed Egyptologists, ensuring a deeply enriching and unforgettable tour.

Tour Highlights:

  • Karnak Temple Complex: Step into the largest religious site in the ancient world. Walk through the breathtaking Great Hypostyle Hall, surrounded by 134 towering columns.
  • Luxor Temple: Located in the heart of the city, this temple is a stunning testament to the reign of Amenhotep III and Ramses II.
  • The Avenue of Sphinxes: Walk the path of the Pharaohs on this newly restored ancient road, lined with hundreds of sphinx statues.
