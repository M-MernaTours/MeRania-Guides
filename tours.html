The Pyramids

Giza plateau — home of the Great Pyramid, Khafre and Menkaure; iconic archaeological site and one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World.

Sakkara

Site of the Step Pyramid of Djoser and expansive necropolis complex that reveals the dawn of pyramid architecture in Egypt.

Memphis

Ancient capital of Egypt — visit ruins, colossal statues and learn about Old Kingdom administration and art.

Dahshur

Known for the Bent and Red Pyramids — an important site illustrating experimentation in early pyramid design.

National Museum of Civilization

Comprehensive museum showcasing Egypt's long cultural history from prehistoric eras to modern times.

Grand Egyptian Museum

Massive modern museum near Giza housing Tutankhamun’s treasures and thousands of ancient artifacts.

Luxor

Valley of the Kings, Karnak and Luxor Temples — the heart of New Kingdom religious and funerary architecture.

Aswan

Nile islands, Philae Temple and Nubian culture; gateway to southern Egypt and beautiful river landscapes.

Western Oases

Bahariya, Siwa, Farafra — desert oases with unique landscapes, ancient sites and cultural experiences.

Alexandria

Mediterranean port city with Greco-Roman heritage: libraries, forts, and coastal scenery.
