The Ultimate 12-Day Egypt Masterpiece

A grand private odyssey through the ages. Discover the entire soul of the Nile valley, from Greco-Roman Alexandria to the Sun Temples of Abu Simbel, led by bespoke archaeology experts.

Days 1–4: Cairo, Giza & Greco-Roman Alexandria

  • Private expedition to Giza Pyramids, the Sphinx, and the Grand Egyptian Museum (GEM).
  • Journey to the Origins: Saqqara Step Pyramid and the Royal Mummies Hall at NMEC.
  • Full-day coastal escape to **Alexandria**, exploring the Citadel of Qaitbay and Roman ruins.
  • Spiritual walk through Saladin’s Citadel and the vibrant streets of Old Cairo.

Days 5–6: The Golden Age of Thebes (Luxor)

  • Pilgrimage to the monumental East Bank: Karnak Temple and Luxor Temple at sunset.
  • Secrets of the Afterlife: The Valley of the Kings, Hatshepsut Temple, and Colossi of Memnon.
  • Bespoke archaeology guidance through every tomb, tailored to your interests.

Days 7–9: The Eternal River (Luxury Nile Cruise)

  • Embark on your private guided Nile Cruise odyssey from Luxor to Aswan.
  • Visit the temple of Horus at Edfu and the unique twin temple of Kom Ombo.
  • Traditional Nile Felucca sailing and Nubian culture exploration on the water.

Days 10–12: Southern Wonders & Grand Finale

  • Island pilgrimage to Philae Temple and the monumental High Dam of Aswan.
  • Iconic early morning expedition to the Sun Temples of Abu Simbel at sunrise.
  • Final morning of relaxation and private VIP transfer for your final departure.
